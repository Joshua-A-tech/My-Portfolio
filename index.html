About Me

I'm a passionate Computer Science student and aspiring cybersecurity specialist currently pursuing my degree at Masinde Muliro University of Science and Technology while simultaneously completing a professional Cybersecurity certification through ALX.

My work focuses on identifying digital vulnerabilities and designing secure, user-friendly applications that address real-world risks. Driven by a desire to make technology safer for all, I aim to become a leading security analyst and educator, particularly for communities most vulnerable to cyber threats.
